Recommended Reading

The Gift of Fear by Gavyn De Becker; Protecting the Gift by Gavin De Becker; Strong on Defense by Sanford Strong; In an Unspoken Voice: How the Body Releases Trauma and Restores Goodness by Peter A. Levine; Hands Off: A Self-Defense Manual for Women by Matt and Judy Thomas.

Statistics

  • 1 in 4 women experience severe intimate partner violence.
  • Women from low-income households experience 6 times the rate of intimate partner violence.
  • Women experiencing intimate partner violence are at higher risk for chronic health conditions.
